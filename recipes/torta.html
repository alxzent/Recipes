Torta Cubana

For the longest time, my favorite food was tortas, specifically torta cubanas. Their compact nature inticed me, as I was and still am a huge meat lover. This torta comes with all the perks of other tortas, the most supreme of all tortas. It's packed with eggs, ham, cheese, milanesa, sausage, beans, chorizo, veggies, and some avocado. Young Alex could not get enough of this angelical concoction. Today, I get to share that experience with you.

Ingredients Needed

  • Bolio
  • Milanesa
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • Mayo
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Beans
  • Chorizo
  • Jalapenos

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Heat up a pan and add your preferred oil to cook the meat.
  3. Toss in your ground beef and cook for 6 minutes. Around3 minutes, add in the sausage
  4. While the meat is cooking, chop up the onion in cubes.
  5. Once 6 minutes have passed, turn off the pan and prepare your baking dish
  6. In a 8 inch baking dish, line the dish with tomato sause and add a layer of the pasta.
  7. Add more sause, some onion, some meat and sprinkle cheese.
  8. Create another layer with the pasta and repeat the previous step until the pan is full.
  9. Once the pan is full, top the pan with cheese and basil. Cover with aluminium
  10. Insert the pan in the pre-heated oven and let it cook for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Remove the pan from the oven after the allocated time and let cool for 5-10 minutes.
  12. Once cool, prepare to serve and enjoy.
